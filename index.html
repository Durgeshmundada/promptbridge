How It Works

From rough idea to sharper AI output in three simple steps.

PromptBridge stays out of your way. You type normally, it enriches behind the scenes, and your AI gets a dramatically better prompt with structure, safety, and context built in.

1

Type prompt

Start naturally on ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or Perplexity. No prompt engineering expertise required.

2

PromptBridge enriches

It classifies intent, matches templates across DIRECT / PARTIAL / GENERATE zones, redacts sensitive data, and clarifies ambiguity.

3

Better output

Your AI receives a cleaner, safer, more structured prompt designed for more useful and more reliable results.
